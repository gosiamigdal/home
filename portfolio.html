Trip planner

SimpleSanFrancisco.com lets you create personalized guide which you can easily use during your San Francisco trip. It looks great on all devices and it is also easy to print. I love travelling and my goal was to help another travellers to easily find best activities in the city of Golden Gate Bridge. It has weather and Facebook APIs integrated.

Events finder

Findevents.migdal.pl allows you to easily find events for your free time. I uses your primary Google Calendar. It is Beta version, so it is still lots to work on. In the next version I am going to take timezones into account and allow users to specify categories of events which they want to attend.
